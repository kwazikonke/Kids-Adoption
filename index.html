Why Donate?

To help and support infants, toddlers, and other adopted, abandoded babies.The abandondment of babies in South Africa is a common issue and it is growing. The reality is that when you donate your money, you help others who need it and when you donate or put up for an adoption of an infant you give an innocent soul a chance to live.

Save abandoned babies
Most of these kids are left to die, or abandoned in dustbins, under bridges, public toilets. We don’t live in a perfect world, and there’s never going to be a perfect time to abandoded your baby, but the Kids-Donation is always there to save abd take care of your baby.
Help families in need of children
Helping families who are in need of children. After the baby is placed on the box or brought to the secrete location, the Kids-Donation staff takes good care of the baby until the adoption parents comes and take the baby.
Bringing children home and giving them a sense of belonging
When you donate money to charity, you create opportunities to meet new people who believe in the same causes that inspire you. That, and making a real impact on those causes, can infuse your everyday life with more meaning. If you’ve been stuck in a rut, whether personally or professionally, sometimes the simple act of donating cash can do the trick and reinvigorate your life.
